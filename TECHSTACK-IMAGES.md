## Format Requirements
- **Format**: WebP (preferred) or PNG
- **Size**: 512x512px or larger (will be scaled down)
- **Background**: Transparent or white (will be displayed on dark background)

## How to Add Images
1. Download/create the logo images in WebP format
2. Place them in `/public/images/` directory
3. Make sure the filename matches exactly (lowercase, with extension)
4. The TechStack component will automatically load and display them
